Create Some Space

Designate a workspace at home for those late nights. This can anywhere you have access to a flat surface and adequate lighting adequate lighting that allows you concentrate-a desk, your kitchen table, a reading chair, just not your bed!This helps your mind and body understand when you're in working mode and allows you to more effectively transition to "home" mood when you're done
